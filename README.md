# Customer Churn & Retention Analytics
Analyzing bank customer churn using Python, SQL, and machine learning to understand why customers leave and how businesses can improve retention.

## Dataset Story

Customer churn is an important business problem for banks because losing existing customers can directly affect revenue, customer lifetime value, and long-term growth.

For this project, I started with a raw bank customer dataset containing 10,000 customer records. Each row represents one customer, and the columns describe customer profile and banking behavior, such as credit score, geography, gender, age, account balance, estimated salary, active membership, and churn status.

The most important column in this dataset is `churn`, which tells us whether a customer left the bank or stayed.

- `1` means the customer churned
- `0` means the customer stayed

By analyzing this dataset, the goal is to find patterns behind customer churn and later build a machine learning model that can help the bank identify customers at risk of leaving.
